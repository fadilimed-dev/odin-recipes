Lasgna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a hearty and comforting dish, perfect for family dinners. Layers of rich tomato sauce, creamy cheese, and perfectly cooked pasta come together to create a meal everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 jar (24 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, sauté the onion and garlic until softened. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta, half of the mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. In a 9x13 baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Layer 4 lasagna noodles, followed by the cheese mixture, meat sauce, and repeat until you have 3 layers.
  7. Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 10 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
